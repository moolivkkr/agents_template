## 7. Key Statistics (from documentation)

- **1,800+** predefined classifiers and policy templates
- **90+** countries covered by predefined policies
- **160+** regions with specific regulatory coverage
- **1,700+** classifiers stated in some marketing materials (1,800+ in more recent materials)
- **5** user risk levels in Risk-Adaptive Protection
- **32** maximum fields per table in database fingerprinting
- **25 MB** maximum OCR file size, **5 KB** minimum
- **255** character maximum for key phrases
- **99.99%** uptime SLA for SaaS deployment (AWS-hosted)
